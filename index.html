about us

Who We Are

Omnifood is India's leading 100% vegetarian food delivery service. Our mission is to bring healthy, delicious, and culturally rich meals to your doorstep. With a strong belief in clean eating, Indian traditions, and mindful cooking, we serve only freshly-prepared food made with love by regional chefs across India.

  • Pure Vegetarian Meals
  • Traditional Recipes & Modern Nutrition
  • Fast, Reliable Delivery

All our vegatables are fresh, organic and local. Animals are raised without added hormones or antibiotics. Good for your health, the environment, and it also tastes better.
